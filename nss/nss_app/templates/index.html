WHAT ARE WE?

The Motto of NSS "Not Me But You", reflects the essence of democratic living and upholds the need for self-less service. NSS NIT Surat is the NIT Surat chapter of the National Service Scheme, institutionalized under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports Govt. of India. It was established in Sept' 2017 and since then, we have been actively instilling the importance of social responsibility in the minds of students, with sole aim to motivate students at NIT & IIIT Surat to indulge in nation building activities through various events and projects which are aimed towards the benefit of people in and around Surat City. We share the belief that such activities are almost always means of great satisfaction and joy. We have a volunteer base of around 200 students belonging to various disciplines and degrees of NIT and IIIT Surat. Each member committed to work for the welfare and benefit of the society by having a sense of gratitude & responsibility towards our citizen and our country. We work on a diverse range of social and environmental issues like blood donation, eye checkup camp at colleges, River cleanup, binary Planation and many other events.

WHAT WE DO?

The main objectives of National Service Scheme (NSS) are : 1) Understand the community in which they work 2) Understand themselves in relation to their community 3) Identify the needs and problems of the community and involve them in problem-solving 4) Develop among themselves a sense of social and civic responsibility 5) Utilise their knowledge in finding practical solutions to individual and community problems 6) Develop competence required for group-living and sharing of responsibilities 7) Gain skills in mobilising the community participation 8) Acquire leadership qualities and democratic attitudes 9) Develop capacity to meet emergencies and natural disasters and 10) Practise national integration and social harmony.

MOTTO

The Motto of NSS "Not Me But You", reflects the essence of democratic living and upholds the need for self-less service. NSS helps the students development & appreciation to other person's point of view and also show consideration towards other living beings. The philosophy of the NSS is a good doctrine in this motto, which underlines on the belief that the welfare of an individual is ultimately dependent on the welfare of the society as a whole and therefore, the NSS volunteers shall strive for the well-being of the society.

LOGO

The logo for the NSS has been based on the giant Rath Wheel of the world famous Konark Sun Temple (The Black Pagoda) situated in Orissa, India. The Red & Blue colors contained in the logo motivate the NSS Volunteers to be active & energetic for the nation-building social activities. The wheel portrays the cycle of creation, preservation and release and signifies the movement in life across time and space, The wheel thus stands for continuity as well as change and implies the continuous striving of NSS for social change.
